About Me

"A journey of thousand miles begins with a single step."

Born in Ames, Iowa and raised in South Korea, I've always had a passion for creativity and design. As a freshman in high school, I began filling my sketchbook with drawings and paintings, and knew that my journey towards a career in the arts had begun. While I initially intended to transfer to an art school, I eventually discovered my interest in Informatics - the science of data, information, and knowledge - and decided to pursue a major in that field with a cognate in Graphic Design. Currently, I'm a senior at Indiana University Bloomington.

As I progressed through college, I found that a combination of web development and graphic design offered the best path forward for me. I believe that these skills, combined with my passion for creativity, will allow me to make a meaningful impact in my future career. One quote that has always resonated with me is by Pablo Picasso: "Everything you can imagine is real." I take this to heart, and strive to be as creative and imaginative as possible in all of my work.

My journey towards a career in design has been an ongoing process, and while it may have started with a single step, it has been made up of many steps along the way. Through hard work, dedication, and a commitment to creativity, I am confident that I can achieve my goals and make a positive impact in the world of design.
